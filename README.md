This repository contains advanced analog electronics projects, labs, and simulations completed in ELE504 at Toronto Metropolitan University. The course expanded on analog circuit analysis with a focus on operational amplifiers, oscillators, filters, signal conditioning, and practical electronic system design.

Key concepts explored include:

Linear and non-linear op-amp circuits
Instrumentation amplifiers
Active filters and tuned amplifiers
Oscillators and signal generators
Power output stages
Analog circuit design methodologies

The course emphasized practical engineering design through simulation, hardware implementation, and a major design project involving real-world electronic applications.
